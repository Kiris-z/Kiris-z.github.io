二维正态分布

$(X,Y)\sim N(\mu_1,\mu_2;\sigma_1^2,\sigma_2^2;\rho)(\sigma_1>0,\sigma_2>0,|\rho|<1)$
$$
f(x,y)=\frac{1}{2\pi \sigma_1\sigma_2\sqrt{1-\rho^2}}\cdot \exp{-\frac{1}{2(1-\rho^2)}[\frac{(x-\mu_1)^2}{\sigma_1^2}-2\rho\frac{(x-\mu_1)(x-\mu_2)}{\sigma_1\sigma_2}+\frac{(y-\mu_2)^2}{\sigma_2^2}]}
$$
